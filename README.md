# 📊 Sales-in-Egypt-Dashboard - Power BI Project

## 🔹 Overview
This interactive dashboard provides a detailed analysis of sales performance, focusing on profit, customer behavior, and regional distribution. It enables users to explore data dynamically and gain actionable insights.

---

## 🔹 Objectives
- Monitor sales and profit performance  
- Analyze customer contribution to revenue  
- Track monthly sales trends  
- Evaluate regional performance  
- Support data-driven business decisions  

---

## 🔹 Key Features

### 📌 KPIs
- Total Profit: 32M  
- Units Sold: 61K  
- Number of Customers: 1612  

### 📊 Channel Analysis
- Comparison between Retail (41%) and Wholesale (59%)  

### 📈 Time Analysis
- Monthly sales trend to identify performance patterns  

### 🌍 Regional Analysis
- Profit distribution across cities (Alexandria, Cairo, Suez, Hurghada, Port Said)  

### 👥 Customer Insights
- Top customers ranked by value contribution  

### 🗺️ Sales Rep Performance
- Geographic visualization of sales representatives' performance  

---

## 🔹 Filters & Interactivity
The dashboard includes dynamic filters for:
- Sales Channel (Retail / Wholesale)  
- City selection  
- Sales Representatives  

All visuals are interconnected for deeper data exploration.

---

## 🔹 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI  
- Data Modeling  
- DAX  

---

## 🔹 Key Insights
- Wholesale channel contributes the largest share of sales  
- Sales performance varies across months indicating trends  
- Alexandria generates the highest profit among cities  
- A small group of customers drives a large portion of revenue  

---

## 🔹 Business Value
This dashboard helps decision-makers to:
- Identify high-performing regions and customers  
- Improve sales strategies  
- Monitor performance effectively  
- Make faster and data-driven decisions
